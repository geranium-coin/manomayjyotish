August 2022 Aries Horoscope – Next Month’s Aries Horoscope | August Horoscope

Leave a Comment / Uncategorized / By admin

General

The month of August 2022 can give mixed results to the natives with the Aquarius zodiac sign. The presence of planets in your Kundli, especially, Sun, Venus, and Mercury will impact different aspects of your life. In the second week of the month, Venus and Sun will remain positioned in the fourth house in Aries; as a result, you may be assigned new responsibilities at the workplace.

On the other hand, Saturn, the ruling lord of your deeds, being present in its own house may inspire you to work hard, and thus, you may get positive results. The month of August 2022 can be a bit challenging from a health point of view. You are likely to confront health-related problems because of the presence of Mercury,

the ruling of your sixth house, in the twelfth position from its own house. Letâ€™s move forward and know what the month of August 2022 has on a platter for you.

Career

From the career point of view, the month of August 2022 is likely to remain average for Aries. During this period, Saturn, the ruling lord of your tenth house, will stay in your tenth in a retrograde position after transiting from the eleventh house, due to which, you can be seen working hard for the success of your work. In the first half of the month, the work which has gone astray can come back because of the presence of Venus and Sun in your fourth house.

Owing to these two planets, new opportunities for employment will knock at your door. During this time, those natives who are looking for a job are likely to meet success. On the other hand, the period may prove to be approved for those who are associated or employed with foreign companies or those related to overseas business.

There is an excellent possibility that you may be fully rewarded for your labour. In the second half of the month, Sun will transit and form a conjunction with Mercury, leading to the formation of Budhaditya Yoga. During this time, there is a strong possibility of opening new employment opportunities for you.

Finance

If we see things from a financial point of view, the month of August 2022 is likely to be profitable for Aries. During this period, Venus, the ruling lord of your second house, the house of money or family, will reside in your fourth house, the house of comforts, with Sun, and thus, there can be an increase in your material pleasure.

Along with this, you may also have financial gains. At the same time, you are likely to have full benefits of your labour due to the presence of Saturn, the ruling lord of the eleventh house, the house of profits, and tenth house, the house of Karma, in your tenth house. During this period, you can be seen faring well in your career. The income of service people is likely to increase.

Those associated with the business may experience profitable times. However, you are suggested not to borrow or lend money because of the presence of Rahu with Mars in your first house in the same month i.e., August 2022 and if you do so, you may come across several challenges while returning or getting back the money. The period may be favourable for those engaged in foreign business. The natives of this zodiac sign may be successful in saving money and therefore, they will be financially sound.

Health

So far the health of Aries people is concerned, the month of August 2022 is going to give mixed results. During this period, Mercury, the ruling lord of your sixth house, the house of disease, will be present in the twelfth house from its own house and as a result, the natives may confront health-related troubles. You are advised to avoid eating outside during this time. You may also suffer from venereal diseases. Too much exertion can create physical as well as mental problems. In such a situation, take out some time for yourself so that you can have some physical rest, otherwise, you may fall ill.

In this month, you will be seen helping others rather than taking care of yourself due to the aspect of Jupiter on your sixth house, the house of disease and this may impact your health adversely. In this regard, you are advised to pay equal heed to your health as well. In the second half of the month, the Sun will be present in your fifth house, due to which, you may be relieved of prolonged diseases. Seeing improvement in the health of elders, you may feel mentally comfortable.

Love/Marriage/Personal Relations

The first fortnight of August 2022 is likely to be approved from the love angle for Aries. At the beginning of the month, Mercury, the significator of intelligence and communication, will be transiting in the fifth house, the house of children and education. Owing to this very reason, your relationship with your lover can be pleasant.

At the same time, Sun, the ruling lord of your fifth house, will form a conjunction with Venus in your fourth house, the house of comforts, and therefore, you are advised to have control over your language while interacting with your lover or spouse, otherwise, bitterness may crop up in your relations.

Try to sort things out amicably instead of frowning. Material pleasures in married life or love relationships may increase due to the presence of Venus, the benefactor of material pleasures, in your fourth house. The natives of the Aries zodiac sign should listen to the worries of their lover or spouse and try to understand their emotions. If Venus is strongly placed in your horoscope, the relations will bloom.

Family & Friends

During the first fifteen days of the month, your family life may be comfortable due to the transit of Venus and Sun in your fourth house, the house of comforts and mother. During this time, love and affection among family members can increase and positive signs may be visible all around. Family members may ponder over resolving past conflicts through mutual discussions.

You can play a pivotal role to eradicate the problems occurring in your family life with your intelligence and maturity, as a result, a pleasant atmosphere may prevail in the family. Mercury, the ruling of your third house, is transiting in your fifth house, the house of children or education, and as a result,

your understanding with your father, and brothers, & sisters is likely to strengthen. Married natives are likely to be benefitted from in-laws and your relationship with them may turn out better.

Advice

Recite the Bajrang Baan Path and offer Bhog to Lord Hanuman.
Offer Churma to Lord Hanuman.
Donate mustard oil in the Shani temple on Saturday.
Worship the Sun on Sunday.
